RULES

Weekly pin shoots are held on Mondays at 6:30 PM year-round.
(Please arrive a few minutes early to sign up.)

5 shots per station, 1 shot at a time
25 Shots Total
Open sight straight wall pistol cartridge 9mm to 44 Magnum
Steel pins are used

Cost: $6.00 ($2.50 prize / $2.50 club / $1.00 pin setter)

If there is a National Holiday on Monday, the shoot is canceled.
These shoots are open to the public.

Classification Rules
All new shooters will compete in Class 4A for their first shoot.
Their first score will determine their classification:

1A: 0–18
2A: 19–22
3A: 23–24
4A: 25

Advancement
Shooters may advance to a higher classification based on performance:

1A → 2A: After 4 scores of 19 or higher
2A → 3A: After 4 scores of 23 or higher
3A → 4A: After 4 Perfect scores of 25

Must be achieved within a 10-week period.
